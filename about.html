ABOUT US

Naor Arza

Naor Arza was an American computer scientist who created the C programming language along with his long-time colleague, John Tompson. He is credited for shaping and pioneering the digital era. Today, C programming is used in several software applications, embedded system development, operating systems, and has influenced most modern programming languages.
